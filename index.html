Hi! 👋

I develop things for the web

I'm a newbie web developer focused on frontend, but also with backend knowledge, lover of continuous learning and with a taste for design.

Who I am?

I have been a technology journalist for almost a decade in media such as Xataka or Genbeta, in addition to having worked for an association of public transport companies, and recently decided to change profession and become a programmer.

I studied a Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at Codehouse Academy where I learned the foundations of HTML and CSS, programming with JavaScript, and worked with Angular. I also developed projects with Node.js and Express, worked with MySQL and MongoDB databases, used version control with Git, and applied Scrum for agile development.

My goal is to continue learning and improving my skills as a developer, especially in the frontend field with the technologies I have worked with, and to add value with my work.

I have great skills in digital environments due to my professional background and my love for technology, and experience in working remotely and as part of a team. I am curious, committed, optimistic and hardworking.
